Focus: Use the function of AF point in camera

AF is an Auto Focus unlike MF that is Manual Focus.

Imagine when you take a picture of dynamic moment.

You can focus on static subjects accurately, but when there are moving subjects, you should try to take the picture many times or can miss that because the focus is not correct.

You can see several point called AF point on the viewfinder of camera.

There are three types of AF mode; One shot AF, AI Focus(= One Shot + AI Servo), and Al Servo.

First, One shot AF can use when you take a picture of the moveless subject.

You can switch the point that you want to focus on.

Next, when you use AI Servo, if you focus on the subject and press the shutter half, the point of focus moves according to the movement of the subject.

Finally, AI Focus is auto fuction that the camera automatically switches 'One shot' or 'AI Servo' according to the movement of the subject
